Our Mission. Eco friendly products.

Sonority was founded in Leuven, Belgium, by five engineering technology students. Our goal is to redevelop products to have a minimal inpact on the environement. By sourcing resources localy and by selecting eco-friendly materials, Sonority tries to improve the environement.

Our Vision. Live greener!

What are our values?

  • We provide maximum audio amplification, for a minimum ecological footprint,
  • We only use renewable and reusable resources,
  • We create an added value to society, economy, and the environment.
